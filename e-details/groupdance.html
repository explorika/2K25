  • Team Size: Each group must consist of a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 6 participants.
  • Time Limit: Each performance must be between 3 to 6 minutes long. Exceeding the time limit may lead to point deduction.
  • Music Submission: Teams must submit their music tracks at least 48 hours before the event in the specified format (MP3/WAV).
  • Props and Costumes: Props are allowed but must be brought by the participants. Use of hazardous or messy materials (like water, fire, powder) is prohibited.
  • Theme and Style: Any dance style is allowed (e.g., classical, hip-hop, freestyle), but the choreography should be appropriate for a general audience.
  • Decency Clause: Vulgar or offensive moves, gestures, or costumes will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Reporting Time: All teams must report to the venue at least 1 hour before their scheduled performance for registration and stage rehearsal (if any).
  • Judging Criteria: Performances will be judged based on choreography, coordination, expressions, creativity, and overall impact.
  • Backstage Conduct: Participants must maintain discipline backstage and follow instructions from event coordinators.
  • Decision of Judges: The decision of the judges will be final and binding. No objections or disputes will be entertained.
