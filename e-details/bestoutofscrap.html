💃 Waste Wounders
(Best Out of Scrap)

Showcasing creativity, rhythm, and cultural beats on the grand Explorika stage!

📖 About the Event

A fusion of innovation and sustainability, this event turns discarded scraps into stunning creations that redefine the boundaries of imagination and resourcefulness.

🕒 Schedule

  • Date: 19th April 2025
  • Time: 2:30 PM
  • Venue: SJB-2, LH-11.

📋 Guidelines & Rules

  • Theme: Open theme—create freely with no topic restrictions.
  • Eligibility: Open to all UG/PG students of Kishkinda University; teams of 3 members only.
  • Materials: Only waste/discarded materials allowed (e.g., bottles, wires, newspapers, fabric, broken parts). New materials not allowed except glue, tape, scissors, etc.
  • Time Limit: 90 minutes.
  • Presentation: 1–2 minute explanation of the idea, materials used, and purpose of the product.
  • Safety: No hazardous, sharp, or flammable items. Maintain cleanliness.
  • Disqualification: Use of new materials, exceeding time, or breaking rules.
  • Goal: Turn “Waste to Wealth” by reusing and reinventing!
