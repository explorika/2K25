Ethnic Fashionista Competition - Event Plan

Event Overview

The Ethnic Fashionista Competition is a celebration of traditional and ethnic attire through a three-round ramp walk. Each participant will showcase their unique cultural wear in various styles, focusing on creativity, confidence, and authenticity.

Event Rounds

Round 1: Traditional Ethnic Attire

Participants will wear authentic ethnic clothing that represents their culture or heritage, highlighting the richness and history of their tradition.

Round 2: Fusion Ethnic Attire

Participants will present modern adaptations of traditional outfits, blending cultural elements with contemporary styles. Judging will be based on originality, creativity, and balance between tradition and modernity.

Round 3: Formal Ethnic Attire

Participants will showcase formal ethnic wear such as sarees, kurta suits, or similar attire that reflects cultural refinement. This round highlights poise, grace, and overall presentation.

Round 4: Questionnaire Round

Judges will ask participants culturally themed questions to gauge their understanding, wit, and connection to their ethnic roots.

Judging Criteria

  • Authenticity and cultural representation (Round 1)
  • Creativity and originality (Round 2)
  • Elegance and presentation (Round 3)
  • Knowledge, confidence and relevance (Round 4)

Event Rules and Regulations

  • Team Size: Individual participation.
  • Dress Code: Strictly ethnic attire reflecting cultures.
  • Time Adherence: Participants must adhere to the allocated time which includes each round of min. 30 sec, max. 1 min.
  • Behaviour: Respectful behaviour is mandatory on and off the stage.
  • Punctuality: All participants must arrive 30 min before the event starts.
  • Judges Decision: The judges' verdict will be final and binding.

Participant Identity and Scoring

Each participant will receive a unique identification number for the competition. Scores will be given for every round, with the final score being the total of all rounds. Top scorers will advance based on the overall aggregate.

Closing and Announcements

After the ramp walk rounds, scores will be tallied, and winners will be announced based on their cumulative scores across all rounds. The event will conclude with the crowning of Mr. and Miss Ethnic as the top performers.
