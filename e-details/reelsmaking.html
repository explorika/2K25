    Rules and Regulations

    1. The duration of the reel must not exceed 90 seconds.
    2. Videos should be submitted in MP4 format, preferably in vertical (9:16) orientation.
    3. All content must be original and created solely by the participant.
    4. Submissions must maintain decency and be appropriate for all age groups. Offensive language, gestures, or controversial content are strictly prohibited.
    5. Entries must be submitted on time; late submissions will not be accepted.
    6. Reels should be submitted via Email and WhatsApp with proper access settings enabled.
    7. Each video file should be named as: Participant Name, Branch.
    8. Reels will be judged on:
      • Creativity
      • Originality
      • Relevance to theme
      • Visual appeal
      • Message clarity
    9. The decision of the judges is final and binding.
    10. The organizing team reserves the right to reject any entry that does not comply with the guidelines.
    11. Only original content created by the participant will be accepted. Use of movie clips, meme templates, or third-party content is strictly prohibited.
    12. The video must be of good visual and audio quality; unclear or poorly edited entries may be rejected.

    Eligibility

    1. The competition is open to all branches.
    2. Participants must carry their college ID card for verification.
    3. Participation is open to both individuals and groups, with a maximum of 6 members allowed per group.
